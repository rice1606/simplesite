My Course in Web & Digital Ad Analytics

Currently, I am in a course at UNC Monfort College of Business that focuses on Web & Digital Ad Analytics, which is becoming increasingly important in this day and age. If you understand the importance of Google Analytics, it can be a powerful and game-changing tool.

Why Google Analytics?

In today's digital landscape, understanding your audience is key to driving growth. That's where Google Analytics comes in! Whether you're looking to optimize a website, boost conversions, or fine-tune marketing efforts, GA provides the data you need to make informed, strategic decisions.

What's new with Google Analytics 4 (GA4)?

  • GA4 is the new, default Google Analytics platform, which replaced Universal Analytics (UA) in 2023.
  • Event-based data collection: Everything is recorded as an event in GA4, whereas UA had a session-based model. This provides a deeper understanding of user behavior.
  • Customizability: GA4 allows custom events and parameters for more detailed tracking and analysis, unique to your specific business and what you would like to analyze.
  • AI-powered insights: Automated alerts and predictions to help you stay ahead of trends.

Why should you care?

  • User insights: Detailed information that allows you to know who your audience is, how they behave and why, and what engages them the most. Thus, you are able to better connect with and cater to your specific audience.
  • Conversion tracking: Track your leads, sales, and other key actions to maximize ROI.
  • Real-time monitoring: Get live data on traffic and user engagement, so youâ€™re always aware of the traffic on your site.

Practical tips:

  • Understand your traffic sources: Find out where your visitors are coming from to double down on what works, and learn what areas need help.
  • Optimize for conversions: Use data to improve your user experience and fine-tune high-traffic pages.
  • Measure content performance: Discover which pages and campaigns are resonating the most with your audience.
