A Bit About Me

I am a Senior Business Marketing Student at the University of Northern Colorado. I am interested in marketing because it is both creative and analytical, and I like the idea of using both the left and right side of my brain in my work. Outside of school, I bartend at a local brewery and manage their social media for my internship to graduate. Here are some of my interests:

Graphic Design

I like to think of Graphic Design almost like a digital scrapbook. It is a lot of fun to experiment with different elements to make things look visually appealing. I primarily have experience with programs such as Canva and Procreate.

Social Media Marketing

Currently, my goal after college is to continue on a path where I can stay behind the bar while also managing social media.

Music

I have always had a passion for music. I spent several years as a part of auditioned choirs and have even travelled to Ireland to perform in different cathedrals.
